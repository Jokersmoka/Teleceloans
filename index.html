Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Flex Loan? ▼
Flex Loan is a mobile loan available on Telecel Cash that allows eligible subscribers to borrow money directly into their Telecel Cash wallet. It is funded by First Atlantic Bank (FAB).
Who is eligible to apply? ▼
You must be 18 years or older and have an active Telecel Cash wallet. Eligibility is also based on your account history and usage.
What are the fees and terms? ▼
The loan term is 30 days. We charge a service fee of 8.9% per month. A late payment penalty of 12.5% applies if the loan is not repaid on time.
How do I repay my loan? ▼
You can repay manually via the Telecel Cash menu or allow automatic deduction from your wallet on the due date. Partial repayments are also allowed.
